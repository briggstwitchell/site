Welcome to my personal site

Hi! My name is Briggs Twitchell. I'm a student at Northeastern University pursuing a masters in computer science. My areas of study include software development and data science.

Before studying CS, I worked as a CPA at a regional firm in New England. My background in accounting has given me the tools to perform financial analysis, and I'd like to leverage this to understand how machine learning can be applied to solve business problems.

I have many interests, ranging from music to moral philosophy to statistical analysis. I love to spend my time reading non-fiction, meditating, enjoying the outdoors, and practicing Effective Altruism.

I also love to create and record music. It has a special place in my heart. I mostly play guitar but am familiar with many other instruments, including bass, keyboard, drums, banjo, and violin. Lately, I mostly listen to and play alternative and country music. Some of my favorite artists include Pinegrove, Lomelda, and Watchhouse.
